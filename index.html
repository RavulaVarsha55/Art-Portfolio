About Me

Hi, I'm Varsha — someone who believes colors can tell stories.

For me, art is a little like magic. Just like a spell in Harry Potter, a few strokes of color can transform simple materials into something beautiful and full of life.

I love experimenting with colors, painting, and creating crafts with my hands. Whether it's mixing shades, decorating everyday objects, or turning simple ideas into handmade pieces, the joy for me is in the process of creating.

My art isn't about perfection - it's about curiosity, creativity, and the happiness that comes from making something with your own hands.

Welcome to my colorful little world.
